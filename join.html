Prospective Members

Join Troy VEX Robotics

Open to all Troy High School cadets enrolled in NJROTC. No prior experience required. Just show up ready to work.

What to Expect
Who Can Join Any Troy High School cadet enrolled in NJROTC. All grades welcome.
Experience None required. Most members joined with zero robotics background. We teach building, programming, and strategy from scratch.
Time 3 to 5 hours per week during build season. Competition weekends require travel. Summer sessions are optional.
Season Year-round. Build season runs September through December. Competitions run January through April. Summer is for prototyping and skill development.
Competitions Local scrimmages, SoCal regionals, JROTC Nationals, and the VEX World Championship for qualified teams.
Skills Mechanical engineering, C++ programming, CAD, game strategy, and working under pressure as a team.
Location Room 401, Troy High School, 2200 E Dorothy Ln, Fullerton, CA 92831
Why Join
01

Compete at the Highest Level

JROTC National Champions. Four World-qualified teams. You will compete against the best programs in the country alongside people who take it seriously.

02

Real Engineering Skills

Hands-on work with mechanical design, C++ programming, and CAD. The skills you build here carry into college coursework and engineering careers.

03

Alumni Who Go Far

Troy VEX alumni have interned at Boeing, JPL, and Raytheon and been accepted to Yale, Stanford, and Carnegie Mellon. The program opens doors.

Join Troy VEX Robotics

Troy VEX is open to all Troy High School cadets enrolled in NJROTC. No prior experience needed — just the drive to compete, build, and win.

What to Expect

Who Can Join Any Troy High School cadet currently enrolled in NJROTC — all grades welcome.
Experience Required None. The majority of our members joined with zero robotics background. We teach everything from the ground up — building, programming, and strategy.
Time Commitment 3–5 hours per week during build season. Competition weekends require travel. Summer includes optional prep and prototyping sessions.
Season Year-round — build season runs Sep through Dec, competitions run Jan through Apr, and summer is used for early prototyping and skill development.
Competitions Local scrimmages, SoCal regionals, JROTC Nationals, and the VEX World Championship for qualified teams.
What You'll Learn Mechanical engineering, programming (C++), CAD, strategic game analysis, project management, and how to work under pressure as a team.
Location Troy High School — Room 401, 2200 E Dorothy Ln, Fullerton, CA 92831

Compete at the Top

We are JROTC National Champions and consistently field World-qualified teams. You'll compete against the best programs in the country — and win.

Why Sponsor Us?

Proven Success: Troy High School is home to Signature Event Winners and top-of-the-line VEX performers. Our 98601Y team is #5 in the entire world for skills. Our A, and C teams have won Division Finalist @ JROTC Nationals.

Direct Impact: 100% of donations go to parts, travel, and student development.

Community Partnership: Troy VEX does its service to the community and holds summer camps amongst other events to boost the local community.

Legacy: Troy VEX is more than 50% freshman who are fresh into their journey of robotics and STEM. Your donations help fuel the next generation of STEM engineers. Our leadership and upperclassmen have interned or been accepted to prestigious summer camps such as Yale Young Global Scholars, COSMOS, Stanford Summer Session, Boeing, Raytheon, Carnegie Mellon, Peraton, Algoverse AI, and JPL @ Caltech.

Why Partner With Us?

Proven Success

Troy High School is home to the top performers and JROTC National Champions. Our 98601Y team is top ten in the world for skills. Our Y, C, and X teams are qualified for the World level. Triple crown and multiple Division Finalists titles at JROTC Nationals.

Direct Impact

100% of donations go to parts, travel, and student development. We are more than 50% freshmen, so your support fuels the start of many STEM journeys.

Student Legacy

Our leadership and upperclassmen have interned or been accepted to prestigious summer camps such as Yale Young Global Scholars, COSMOS, Stanford Summer Session, Boeing, Raytheon, Carnegie Mellon, and JPL @ Caltech. We have personally sent 5 members to Boeing.

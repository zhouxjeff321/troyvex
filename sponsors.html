Why Partner With Us?

Proven Success

Troy High School is home to Signature Event Winners and top-of-the-line VEX performers. Our 98601Y team is #5 in the entire world for skills, and A & C teams have won Division Finalist @ JROTC Nationals.

Direct Impact

100% of donations go to parts, travel, and student development. We are more than 50% freshmen, so your support fuels the start of many STEM journeys.

Student Legacy

Our leadership and upperclassmen have interned or been accepted to prestigious summer camps such as Yale Young Global Scholars, COSMOS, Stanford Summer Session, Boeing, Raytheon, Carnegie Mellon, and JPL @ Caltech.

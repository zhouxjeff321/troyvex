Why give?

Troy VEX Robotics transforms curiosity into real-world skills. Your donation helps students access tools, travel to competitions, and gain mentorship that prepares them for college and careers in engineering, programming, and design.

  • Real outcomes: State and national finalists, build awards, and hands-on STEM experience.
  • Direct impact: Every dollar goes to parts, travel, registration, and outreach to local schools.
  • Community: Showcase your organization as a supporter of local education and workforce development.

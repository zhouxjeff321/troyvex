Our Story

Building a Legacy.█

From eleven cadets to a global powerhouse, this is the journey of Troy VEX Robotics.

Explore
60+ Members
7 Teams
2023 Est.
Chapter I

2023

11 cadets, 1 garage. Aaron Li and Kausthubh Veldanda rallied a tight-knit crew. This small, tight-knit family provided the program the values and drive we live by today.

Chapter II

2024

The hard work paid off. In a defining season, the team secured 2 Division Finalist Titles at JROTC Nationals. The program doubled in size to 35.

Chapter III

2025

Led by student leaders Ryan Zhou, Krishna Sivakumar, and Avijeet Singh, the program headed to new heights. 60+ members, 7 teams. 4 World qualifications. The largest and strongest VEX JROTC organization in the nation.

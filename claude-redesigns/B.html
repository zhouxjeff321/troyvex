The story

Honor, courage & commitment.

Three years ago, eleven cadets started a robotics program out of a garage at Troy High School. Today, Troy VEX is the largest and most decorated JROTC VEX program in the country — and the Navy JROTC values carry into everything we build and every competition we enter.

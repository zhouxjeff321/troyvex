Chapter I

The Spark

It began with a vision. Aaron Li and Kausthubh Veldanda rallied a tight-knit crew of 13 cadets. They weren't just a team; they were a family, united by a curiosity for engineering and a drive to compete.

2024
Chapter II

The Rise

The hard work paid off. In a defining season, the team secured 2 Division Finalist Titles at JROTC Nationals. This victory wasn't just metal and plastic; it was proof that Troy VEX had arrived.

Now
Chapter III

The Expansion

The torch has passed to a new generation: Ryan Zhou, Krishna Sivakumar, and Avijeet Singh. Today, we are one of the largest robotics organizations in the world, fostering talent across seven distinct squads.

Our Teams

In 2023, Aaron Li and Kausthubh Veldanda founded Troy VEX Robotics. The original crew of 13 members found were a tight-knit family. Aaron and Kausthubh led the team for the 2024 season, bringing home 2 Division Finalist Titles at JROTC Nationals, before passing down the title to Ryan Zhou, Krishna Sivakumar, and Avijeet Singh.

Troy VEX has made a long way from its humble beginnings. From 13 cadets to 72, Troy VEX Robotics is one of the biggest robotics organizations in the entire world. There are currently 7 teams, led by some of the most talented leaders in Orange County.

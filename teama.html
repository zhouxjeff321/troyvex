About the Team

Team 98601A - Aegis was founded by Ryan Zhou and Lucas Wang. The main goal of the team is to make robotics accessible and to make robotics fun and educational.

We use a lever bot where we can store multiple balls at once and shoot it all at once with a press of a button.

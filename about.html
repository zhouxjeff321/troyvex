About Troy VEX Robotics

A proud NJROTC robotics program building the next generation of engineers, leaders, and innovators.

Our Story

Founded 3 years ago at Troy High School in Fullerton, California, Troy VEX Robotics has quickly grown from a small group of curious cadets into the nation's largest JROTC VEX program.

As an NJROTC program, we combine the discipline and leadership values of the Navy Junior Reserve Officers' Training Corps with cutting-edge STEM education. Our cadets don't just build robots‚Äîthey develop critical thinking skills, teamwork, and the confidence to tackle real-world engineering challenges.

With 7 competitive teams and over 72 dedicated students, we've become a force in VEX Robotics competitions, earning recognition at local, regional, and national levels.

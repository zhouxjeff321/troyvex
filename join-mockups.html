Prospective Members

Everything you
need to know.

No prior experience required. Most members joined with zero robotics background. Here is exactly what you are signing up for.

What to Expect
Who Can Join
Any Troy High School cadet enrolled in NJROTC. All grades welcome.
Experience
None required. We teach building, programming, and strategy from scratch.
Time
3 to 5 hours per week during build season. Competition weekends require travel.
Season
Year-round. Build runs Sept–Dec, competitions Jan–Apr, summer for prototyping.
Competitions
Local scrimmages, SoCal regionals, JROTC Nationals, and the VEX World Championship.
Skills
Mechanical engineering, C++ programming, CAD, game strategy, and teamwork under pressure.
Location
Room 401, Troy High School, 2200 E Dorothy Ln, Fullerton, CA 92831.

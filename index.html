Who are we?

The largest JROTC VEX Robotics organization in the nation.

Since the program's founding in 2023, the program has grown from 10 students to 72 students. We currently have a total of 7 teams representing our school. We are an exclusively NJROTC program, making us one of the biggest VEX organizations in the world.

Troy High School's VEX Robotics program provides students with hands-on experience in STEM fields through competitive robotics. Our students design, build, and program robots to compete in VEX Robotics competitions at the highest level.

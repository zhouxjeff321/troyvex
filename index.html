The Nation's Largest JROTC VEX Program

From a humble beginning of 13 cadets to a powerhouse of 72 students, Troy VEX Robotics has grown into a global leader. We are a dedicated family of 7 teams, exclusively representing NJROTC.

Our students don't just build robots; they engineer solutions. Through hands-on experience in STEM, we design, build, and code competitive robots that perform at the highest levels of VEX Robotics competitions.

Meet the Teams

Defining Excellence in JROTC Robotics

Three years ago, a vision took shape within the walls of Troy High School. What began as a modest group of eleven curious cadets has flourished into the nation's premier JROTC VEX Robotics program.

Our foundation is built on the core values of the Navy Junior Reserve Officers' Training Corps: honor, courage, and commitment. We integrate these principles with rigorous STEM education, creating an environment where technical skills meet leadership development.

Today, we field seven competitive teams with over 60+ dedicated members. Our presence is felt at every level of competition, from local scrimmages in Southern California to the National and World level.

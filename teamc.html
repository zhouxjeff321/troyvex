About the Team

Led by Worlds attendees and state finalists, the team leads of 98601C are seasoned veterans looking to dominate the V5 competition.

Jinx uses a front to back bot style. This bot style allows a combination of high storage capacity and fast unload speeds. Their robot is a whopping 12 pounds.
